# Clinivora AI

Clinivora AI is a healthcare intelligence platform being developed to make preventive healthcare more accessible through artificial intelligence, machine learning, and explainable analytics.

The goal of the platform is to help individuals better understand potential health risks, interpret medical information, and access intelligent healthcare insights through transparent and trustworthy AI systems.

## Vision

Healthcare data is often difficult for individuals to understand and act upon. Clinivora AI aims to bridge this gap by combining predictive analytics, explainable AI, and intelligent assistance into a single platform focused on early awareness and informed decision-making.

## Planned Features

### Heart Disease Risk Assessment

Machine learning models designed to estimate cardiovascular risk using clinical indicators and patient information.

### Chronic Kidney Disease Screening

Explainable AI tools that assist in identifying potential CKD-related risk factors and patterns.

### Medical Report Intelligence

Tools for analyzing laboratory reports and presenting key findings in a more understandable format.

### AI Health Assistant

An intelligent assistant designed to answer healthcare-related questions and provide educational guidance.

### Explainable AI

Transparent prediction systems that help users understand how model outputs are generated.

### Health Analytics Dashboard

A centralized dashboard for viewing assessments, reports, and personalized health insights.
